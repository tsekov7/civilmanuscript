FONTLOG for "Civil Manuscript" / „Граждански рѫкописъ“

This font is created by Ts. Tsekov.


Description:
"Civil Manuscript" / „Граждански рѫкописъ“ is a historical Cyrillic font, inspired by late 19th-century Bulgarian typography and orthography 
from the 1879 "Petit manuel pour apprendre la langue Bulgare" by P. V. Gorbanoff, published in 1879, Sofia. 
It includes extended Cyrillic characters and historical glyphs such as ѣ, ѫ, ѭ, faithfully preserved and stylized for modern usage. 

The internatioanl name of the font is „Civil Manuscript“, and the original name in Cyrillic is „Граждански рѫкописъ“.
